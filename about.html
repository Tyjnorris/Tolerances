Welcome To Tolerances

I created this website along with my colleague Vincent Kelly as a way to aid ourselves and colleagues in publishing designs online for our community to view. Our goal when creating this website was to create a creative space where engineering undergraduate's can display their designs to their fellow colleagues and potential employers alike. Contributions to the site are highly encouraged and can be done so by contacting us through the email listed below. Simply email us the following:

  • Full Name
  • University
  • The name of your design
  • A description of what your design features
  • The standards followed (if it is a Computer Automated Design or Technical Drawing)

and we'll post it. Showcase your designs today!
